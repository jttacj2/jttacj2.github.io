Cycle the Continental Divide

Come and cycle the Continental Divide of Montana!

We will begin our journey in Roosville, MT ride for about thirteen hours before stopping to camp in Mt Wright. All camping gear will be provided for you. If you decide to bring your own please make sure that you use all light weight materials. We will then be finishing our trip in Helena the second day spending about another thirteen hours on the trail. Along the route we will be going through deep woods and steep mountains which are native to grizzly bears, elk, moose and mountain lions. Finally, Be prepared to experience thrilling trails and spectacular views!

Information

Route: 380 miles

  • Start: Roosville, MT
  • End: Helena, MT

Basic Requirements:

  • Raingear and cold-weather clothing
  • Some back-country camping experience is prefered but not necessary
  • Food/Water
  • Bear repellent
  • Basic first aid kit

Cost $899
